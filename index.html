Nightingale SMS is a cloud enabled Platform for delivering mission critical sms messages.

 

The platform is built with fail-over in mind and has access to over 173 different locations across the world. Nightingale SMS does not log customer data, instead Nightingale should be considered more like a Knowledge Base or Knowledge engine of sorts that respects customer privacy.

 

 

Nightingale Health

 

 

Nightingale Health is revolutionising the way health systems are accessed. In developing countries there is a high proliferation of gsm enabled mobile phones. Nightingale Health displays how to preform CPR, how to apply a bandage and descriptions of conditions for example: "what is HIV?". With information being delivered within 3 seconds thanks to Nightingale SMS.

 

Nightingale Health is always having new data imported and reviewed by individuals with medical backgrounds. In the future we hope to open up this information so other medical professionals can also contribute towards providing useful health information in translated languages around the world.

Friday Digital Assistant

 

Friday is a Digital Assistant, developing for Windows, Mac and iOS platforms. Friday understands basic commands such as "YouTube cat videos" and "What is the time?", Friday doesn't send data back to us. In-fact the software can run offline without any internet connectivity.

 

Updates can be requested by the community to make Friday even smarter!
